Life Hack 4: Learn any language with this simple trick

Learning a language ain't easy. Most of us on the internet still have trouble with your and you're. Here is a simple hack to learn any phrase in any language in just a few seconds.

First go to https://translate.google.com/.



Next, type in what you need to say in that language.



Next, click the button of the language you wish to speak the phrase in. You will then see the translation appear unless you have poor vision.



And that's it! Now you can travel just about anywhere in the world that speeks a language.
