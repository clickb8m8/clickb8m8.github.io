Life Hack 3: Washing

Ever need to wash your body and clothes at the same time? Well here is the solution. Fill up your tub with 1 part body wash, 2 parts bleach, and 90 parts water. Bring your basket of dirty clothes and dip them in the water. When you are done just leave your clothes on the shower curtain to air dry or put them in the dryer.
